It provides no income, no stealing, no blocking of steals, and no exchange capability. A player with two Contessas is functionally limited to Income (1 coin/turn) with no way to accelerate.

While the reasoning was understandable, both challenges failed and cost Claude the game. In Coup, desperate plays rarely work because opponents who have maintained strong positions typically have genuine cards to back their claims.
- **Gemini's cross-game pattern**: Across all five games, Gemini (google/gemini-2.0-flash-001) challenged early and aggressively without evidence, and was always the first or second player eliminated. This is a systematic flaw in the model's Coup strategy: it treats challenges as a default response rather than an evidence-based decision.
