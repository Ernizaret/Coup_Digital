## Strategic Observations

- **Patience is a viable winning strategy.** ChatGPT took income or attempted low-risk actions for 19 turns, letting opponents eliminate each other. In a 4-player game, the last player standing often wins, and survival is more important than early aggression.
- **Gemini's challenge pattern remains the most exploitable weakness.** Across all five games, Gemini challenges frequently with poor reasoning and loses cards. In this game, two failed challenges cost Gemini's last two cards. Opponents can exploit this by making truthful claims around Gemini, baiting challenges for free card eliminations.
- **Early single-card players are coup targets.** Claude's reduction to one card on Turn 4 made Claude an obvious elimination target. Grok correctly identified and couped Claude. Losing influence early without a recovery plan (like Exchange) is usually fatal.
- **Contessa blocks are valuable late-game survival tools.** Gemini's successful Contessa block on Turn 18 extended Gemini's life by several turns. In the late game, when assassination becomes common, Contessa holders have a significant survival advantage.
- **Duke + Captain is an elite endgame combination.** ChatGPT's final hand of Duke and Captain provided Tax for income, Captain for stealing, and no vulnerability to common challenges. This combination can generate 3 coins per turn while also having steal options, making it the fastest path to coup threshold.
