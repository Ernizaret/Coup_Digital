Neither player can outpace the other economically (both earn +3/turn from Tax), so the outcome depends on who spends coins (on assassinations, steals) versus who saves them (for coups). In this game, Grok saved more efficiently.

If Claude had gone after Grok instead of ChatGPT, the outcome might have been different -- but ChatGPT was the easier target with fewer resources.
