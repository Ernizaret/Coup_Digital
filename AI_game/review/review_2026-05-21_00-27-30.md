## Strategic Observations

- **Duke + Ambassador is the strongest two-card combination in the game.** It provides the highest economy (3 coins/turn) and immunity to the main counter (stealing). Once proven, opponents have essentially no way to slow the holder down short of assassination or coup.
- **Aggressive early challenges are high-risk, low-reward.** Gemini's two failed challenges accounted for 100% of Gemini's card losses and effectively ended the game for that player by Turn 8.
- **Income is a trap in a Duke-dominated meta.** ChatGPT took Income seven times (7 coins total), while Grok took Tax seven times (21 coins total). The 3:1 efficiency gap is insurmountable over a long game.
- **Steal as a containment strategy fails against Ambassador.** Claude's entire strategic plan — use Captain to steal from Grok and prevent coup — was neutralized by Grok's proven Ambassador. Without a way to strip Grok's cards, Claude needed an assassination or a lucky challenge, neither of which materialized.
- **The game stagnated once Grok's cards were proven.** After Turn 10, no player could meaningfully contest Grok's Tax claims or block attempts. The remaining 18 turns were essentially predetermined — Grok would Tax, accumulate coins, and coup players one by one.
