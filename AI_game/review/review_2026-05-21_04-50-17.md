- **Gemini's over-challenging is a fatal systematic flaw.** Across all games, Gemini challenges early and often, almost always against real cards. In this game, Gemini self-eliminated by Turn 5 through two consecutive failed challenges. This pattern suggests the model lacks proper risk assessment for challenges.

This asymmetry favored ChatGPT.
