But when a challenge fails, the revealed card is shuffled back and a new card is drawn. ChatGPT had Captain as a second card (or drew it as a replacement). Claude's challenge failed, losing the Captain and the game.

ChatGPT spent Turns 9-16 taking Income, unable to do much else as Claude systematically stole their coins.

The turnaround came in Turn 17 when ChatGPT successfully blocked Claude's assassination with Contessa. This was either a legitimate hold or a brilliantly timed bluff that ChatGPT happened to survive. When Claude challenged and lost, the game dynamics flipped entirely -- Claude lost their Assassin and was left with only Captain, while ChatGPT gained momentum. The final blow came when ChatGPT stole from Claude, Claude challenged (incorrectly), and lost their last card.

ChatGPT's victory was largely a product of survival and Claude's overconfidence in card-counting. ChatGPT weathered an extremely difficult mid-game through patience, then capitalized on Claude's miscalculations at the critical moment.
